Pricing

Poddigy operates on a Pay-Per-Minute model designed to offer flexibility and affordability.

Each user purchases credits based on the time duration they subscribe for, and credits are deducted at a rate of 1 credit per second consumed on the platform.

Pricing: Rs. 9 for a 1-hour subscription (3600 credits)

Maximum Subscription: Up to 5 hours in a single transaction
